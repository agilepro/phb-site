By closely examining the inner workings of the mind, Mr. Watts exposes the ego-based myths that serve as obstacles to resolving conflicts and to finding happiness and meaning in a polarized world.

With uncommon insight, the author explains how the root of society's and the individual's problems lies in the human mind rather than in social or economic conditions. Based on the exaggerated awareness of separation and differences, rather than of unity and similarities, our dualistic thinking brain totally accepts one side while rejecting the other.

The authentic Self is seen by the author as the key to realizing human potential and to experiencing the greatest high, the love connection between Self and Other that puts the missing piece in place and provides the profound sense of belonging that we all seek.
